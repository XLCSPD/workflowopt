## Profile Settings

### Updating Your Name

1. Go to Settings
2. Find the **Profile** section
3. Edit the **Name** field
4. Click **"Save Profile"**

Your name appears in:
- Your avatar throughout the app
- Observations you create
- Session participant lists

### Email Address

Your email address is displayed but cannot be changed directly. Contact your administrator if you need to update your email.

---

## Password Settings

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to Settings
2. Find the **Password** section
3. Enter your **Current Password**
4. Enter your **New Password**
5. Enter the new password again to **Confirm**
6. Click **"Change Password"**

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols recommended
- Cannot reuse recent passwords

### Forgot Password?
If you can't log in:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions

## Notification Preferences

Control how and when you receive notifications.

### In-App Notifications

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Session Updates** | New sessions, status changes, invitations |
| **Observation Updates** | When observations are added to your sessions |
| **Invitation Updates** | When you're invited to join something |

Toggle each setting on/off as desired.

### Browser Notifications

Receive desktop notifications even when the app isn't focused.

**Enabling Browser Notifications:**
1. Toggle **"Browser Notifications"** on
2. Your browser will ask for permission
3. Click **"Allow"** in the browser prompt

**If Permission Was Denied:**
1. Click the lock icon in your browser address bar
2. Find Notifications settings
3. Change to "Allow"
4. Refresh the page

**Supported Browsers:**
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

## Display Preferences

### Sidebar State
The sidebar remembers your preference:
- **Expanded** - Shows icons and text
- **Collapsed** - Shows icons only (hover for tooltips)

Click the chevron (< >) to toggle.

### Theme
Currently using system default. (Future: Light/Dark mode toggle)

## Account Management

### Viewing Account Info

In Settings, you can see:
- Your email address
- Your organization
- Your role (Participant, Facilitator, Admin)
- Account creation date

### Deleting Your Account

1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **"Delete Account"**

Contact your organization administrator to:

## Privacy & Data

### Data You Control
- Profile information (name)
- Notification preferences
- Training progress

### Data Owned by Organization
- Observations you create
- Session participation
- Workflow contributions

### Data Retention
- Active data retained while account exists
- Deleted accounts: 30-day recovery window
- After 30 days: permanent deletion

## Troubleshooting

### "Cannot save settings"
- Check internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing the page

### "Browser notifications not working"
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure notifications are enabled in OS
- Try a different browser

### "Password change failed"
- Verify current password is correct
- Ensure new password meets requirements
- Try again after a few minutes

### "Settings not loading"
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito/private mode
- Check for browser updates
