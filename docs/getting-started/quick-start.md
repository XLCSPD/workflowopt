# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with ProcessOpt in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

### New Users
1. Navigate to the login page
2. Click **"Sign up"** if you don't have an account
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the link to verify your account

### Invited Users
If you received an invitation email:
1. Click the invitation link in the email
2. Create your password
3. You'll be automatically added to your organization

### Returning Users
1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Sign in"**

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Training

Before participating in waste walk sessions, we recommend completing the training modules:

1. Go to **Training** in the sidebar
2. Start with the first available module
3. Work through videos, articles, and quizzes
4. Track your progress on the training dashboard

**Tip:** Keep the [Waste Cheat Sheet](/training/cheat-sheet) handy during sessions!

---

## Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with:

- **Training Progress** - Your completion status
- **Recent Sessions** - Active and completed waste walks
- **Quick Actions** - Start new sessions or access workflows
- **Analytics Overview** - Key metrics at a glance

---

## Step 4: Join or Create a Session

### As a Participant
1. Wait for a facilitator to invite you to a session
2. Look for active sessions in the **Sessions** tab
3. Click **"Join Session"** to participate

### As a Facilitator
1. Go to **Workflows** and select a process workflow
2. Click the **Play** button to start a new session
3. Or go to **Sessions** â†’ **New Session**
4. Configure session settings and start

---

## Step 5: Tag Waste Observations

During an active session:

1. Navigate through process steps in the workflow view
2. Click on a step to add an observation
3. Select the waste type(s) that apply
4. Add notes describing what you observed
5. Rate the frequency, impact, and ease of improvement
6. Save your observation

---

## Step 6: Review Results

After a session ends:

1. Go to **Sessions** and find the completed session
2. Click **"View Results"** to see the summary
3. Review waste distribution charts
4. Export reports as PDF or PowerPoint
